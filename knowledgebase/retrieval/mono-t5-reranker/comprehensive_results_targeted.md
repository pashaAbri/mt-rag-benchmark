# Comprehensive Reranking Performance Results (Targeted Rewrite)

## Strategy Definitions

- **Targeted Rewrite (No Rerank)**: Retrieval using only the targeted rewrite query (ELSER). No reranking.
- **2-Strategy (L+Q)**: Combines documents retrieved by `lastturn` and `questions` queries. Reranked with MonoT5 (using `targeted_rewrite` query).
- **2-Strategy (L+T)**: Combines documents retrieved by `lastturn` and `targeted_rewrite` queries. Reranked with MonoT5.
- **2-Strategy (Q+T)**: Combines documents retrieved by `questions` and `targeted_rewrite` queries. Reranked with MonoT5.
- **3-Strategy**: Combines documents retrieved by `lastturn`, `questions`, and `targeted_rewrite` queries. Reranked with MonoT5.
